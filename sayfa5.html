Safety Tips for Pranking * Keep it Harmless: Avoid pranks that cause damage to property or real distress.
* Know Your Audience: Don't prank someone who is having a bad day or doesn't share your sense of humor.
* Ensure Safety: Never use hazardous materials or create dangerous situations (e.g., in stairwells or involving traffic).
